UC 1. Customer contact add

1.Administrator navigate Home/Contacts
2.Adm. press button Add contact
3.System show administratorform for adding new contact
4.Adm. fill all of the fields
5.If data isn't valid in any field with *, system show a message
about invalid data under field, and makes Submit button inactive.
6.If all field is valid, adm. press Submit button.
7. System create customer contact and show message Contact created.
